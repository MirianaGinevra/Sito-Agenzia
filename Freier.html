Karlotta Freier

Karlotta Freier is a Brooklyn-based artist with a background in illustration. She graduated from the MFA Illustration as Visual Essay program at the School of Visual Arts. Karlotta also dedicates her time to sharing her knowledge with the next generation of artists. Freier's work has also been exhibited in a variety of solo and group exhibitions,as well as festivals worldwide.
