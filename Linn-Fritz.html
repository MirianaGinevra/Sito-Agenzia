Linn-Fritz

Swedish illustrator, designer and animator Linn Fritz creates charmingly clean imagery in a trademark pastel palette for a wide range of editorial and commercial clients. The progressive nature of Hyper Island led Linn to concentrate on what digital could offer her already well-honed drawings. Linn is the co-founder of Panimation, “a community of equality-driven women, trans and non-binary friends aiming to connect and support each other within the animation industry.”
