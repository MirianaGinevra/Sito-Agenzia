Xena Herrera

The portfolio of Xoana Herrera is an explosion of sumptuous, stylish character scenes dwelling in a world
that, frankly, looks ideal. Xoana trained as a graphic designer in Argentina but over time found herself drawn to the world of illustration after a five-year stint at Buck agency. “Aiming to be innovative is something I feel I can dare to try every time I take a job, and that’s amazing.”
